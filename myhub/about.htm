GRETCHEN'S INN

Your best choice for large parties up to 120 people

Our banquet facility is right next door and shares our parking lot. We'll do the work while you have the fun!

This Bavarian style Dutchtown Landmark with its timber and stucco construction, steeply pitched roof and corner turret began its journey at the behest of another St. Louis institutionâ€¦Anhueser-Busch.


Built in 1913 by Anhueser-Busch, it was christened Busch's Gretchen Inn. Adolphus Busch wanted to show the prohibition movement that beer could be served in a respectable family establishment instead of the typical sawdust floor saloon.


Your current hosts are longtime Dutchtowners, Sue and Marty Luepker, who took on the daunting task of renovating and furnishing the landmark in 1993 after Anhueser-Busch donated the property to the Dutchtown South Neighborhood Association. The Feasting Fox is the symbol of hospitality that graces the side of the Anheuser-Busch Brewery.


In 2001 the building received its long overdue Historic Landmark status.
